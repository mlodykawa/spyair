What changed in this version

This version starts acting like a monitoring tool, not just a map.

🚨

Live alert feed

SpyAir now logs notable events like new military-likely traffic, entries into Poland, and disappearances.

🛡️

Military-likely prioritization

Aircraft with military-style callsigns are highlighted and pushed to the top of the activity feed.

📍

Clickable activity workflow

Click any item in the flight list and jump directly to that aircraft on the map.

Important notes

This is already useful, but still heuristic-based.

Are alerts exact military detections? No. They are based on a first military-likely ruleset, mainly from callsign patterns and position changes.
What should come next? Next step is stronger classification, watchlists, geofences, and a backend alert engine.
Why is this valuable already? Because now you can see live traffic, isolate interesting flights, and watch a running feed of activity changes.
